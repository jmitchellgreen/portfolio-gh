The goal of Parking Proliferation is to highlight the absurdity of the abduance of parking lots in downtown city and town centers. To achieve this, I created a web map app to highlight all parking lots within a given city, town, or neighborhood district (according to OpenStreetMap data). I query all polygons tagged wtih parking and use Turf.js to calculate the area. Built with Maplibre GL JS (open source Mapbox), React, and Tailwind CSS.
