Mitchell is currently a GIS Specialist at GAI Consultants where he works full time for Avangrid Renewables building apps, drawing maps, and managing geospatial data for utility scale solar and wind energy projects.

He studied Geographic Science at James Madison University focusing in applied Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and environmental sustainability.

Some noteworth projects at James Madison include winning 1st place in the Collegiate Wind Competition, creating a custom QGIS Plugin, and receiving JMU's 2020 Geographer of the Year award.

Mitchell is looking to grow as a geospatial software engineer.
